### Changed

- Several Nerfs/Reworks from the 1.19.2 version of Tensura: Starlight, some of which listed below:
- - Ember Remains Buff no longer being based on the in combat effect from mysticism, but rather based on dealing damage.
- - Light Remains can now be acquired with having both Light and Dark greater spirits, however it will NOT be refunded if obtained this way.
- - General nerfs to damage output, armor effectiveness and similar.
