Miner's Grace, Unique Skill

"Blessed hands that shape the world."

Passives

[Passive - Toggle] 50% Increased Mining Speed, and Luck 5

Actives

[Active - Press] Vein Destruction: Target any "Ore" block and destroy all ores up to a limit of 128 that are directly or indirectly connected to the original ore.
Ores destroyed by this effect drop items at a rate of your current fortune total + 25%
Has a functional pickaxe level of diamond (netherite).

[Active - Press] Area Break: Destroy blocks within a 3x3x1 (5x5x1) aoe of the block you target. This can destroy stones, dirts and similar materials.

[Active - Press] Miner's Enchant: Cycle between Fortune, Silk Touch, Efficiency upgrade the enchantments on your current item by +1 up to a limit of 3 (5).

Evolves into Evolved Unique: Chthonian Miner

-----------------------------------

Chthonian Miner, Evolved Unique Skill

"Shape the world through the blessed bounties of the earth."

Passives

[Passive - True] Additionally resist physical damage by 25%
[Passive - Toggle] 75% Increased Mining Speed, and Luck 5

Actives

[Active - Press] Vein Manipulation:
Vein Creation: Create a large vein of random ores in stoney blocks around you. These ores vary from copper to ancient debris, and it occurs over a wide area.

Vein Destruction: Target any block, destroy any matching type of "stone" up to a limit of 256 blocks that are directly or indirectly connected.
Fortune affects this breaking at 2x the usual rate.
Functional Pickaxe level of Low Magisteel

[Active - Press] Earth Shattering: Destroy blocks within a 5x5x3 (7x7x5) area or in a 9x9x1 (11x11x1) area of the original block.
Fortune affects these blocks at a 1.5x the usual rate.

[Active - Press] Chthonian Boon: Apply Fortune and Boon of The Worker, Silk Touch, Efficiency, Unbreaking and Mending, or Drill Tip.
Upgrading existing enchants by +1 (+2) up to a limit of 8 (12)

Malleable, Unique Skill

"To expand and shift for evolution."

Passives

[Passive - True] Increased Mastery and Learning speed by 4 Points (Total 5 without any other bonuses).
[Passive - Toggled] Luck 5
[Passive - In Slot] -0.5% AP, + 2.5% MP

Actives

[Active - Press] Expansion: Spend 5000/5000 mastery to obtain a Random Unique skill of a pre-defined list.
[Active - Press] Malleable Tools: Upgrade existing efficiency on your tool up to level 5 (10).

Unique Skill - [Error/Bug]

The Loophole in rules, the Manifestation of all Errors...

True Passives:
- Thought Acceleration
- ability to see through some weaker illusions
- When attacking the enemy have a chance to steal some of their MP and AP
- Clone Error, they can hurt clones and through that loophole affect the main body for example on unique skills that create a backup body

Active Abilities:
- Steal, you can steal the item held in the hands of the target, this ability uses EP
- Avatars, through the use of loopholes, the loophole being used is that the body can be at multiple places at once each avatar is an "extension" of the main body, all avatars are also connected to the main body, this is akin to a powerful body double.
     - they can also swap positions with their clones
    - in case of death they can revive inside one of their avatars although they would lose some EP as a cost.
- Deceit, they can deceive things to work even if they normally don't for example making a furnace cook things without coal to be used as fuel, they could also make their attacks hit at an abnormal distance such as gaining more reach or going through substitution abilities, the loophole used for the latter on is that if they are struck how could they possibly avoid it?
- Theft, they have a chance to steal skills temporarily, they can take intrinsic, extra and common skills, they are also able to steal unique skills albeit for a much shorter time and with a lower success rate, at max mastery they even have a chance to steal resistances briefly but not nullifications
